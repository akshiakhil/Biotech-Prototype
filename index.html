About Us

Retroindica is a biotechnology company dedicated to revolutionizing healthcare through innovative diagnostic solutions and therapeutic products. Founded in 2018, we specialize in cutting-edge Multiplex Lateral Flow Assays (LFAs) for the rapid detection of viral infections, including respiratory viruses, and various other pathogens. Our focus spans across critical areas of healthcare: diagnostic innovations, cancer diagnostics, and biosimilar development.

Our diagnostic innovations include rapid, reliable LFAs for detecting a wide range of infectious diseases, including respiratory viruses like SARS-CoV-2, Influenza A, and Influenza B, as well as other pathogens. Committed to early cancer detection, we are designing LFAs to identify multiple biomarkers associated with various types of cancer. Leveraging our expertise in antibody engineering, we are developing high-quality biosimilars, particularly monoclonal antibodies, for the treatment of viral infections and cancers.
