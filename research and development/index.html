Our Research and Development Efforts

Retroindica's Research and Development (R&D) efforts are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of diagnostic and therapeutic science. We focus on creating cutting-edge solutions that improve patient outcomes through early detection and advanced treatment options for both infectious diseases and cancers.

Multiplex Lateral Flow Assays for Respiratory Viruses

One of our key areas of innovation is the development of multiplex lateral flow assays designed to detect multiple respiratory viruses in a single test. Our team is at the forefront of validating a comprehensive diagnostic kit that can accurately detect:

  • SARS-CoV-2 (COVID-19)
  • Influenza A and B
  • Respiratory Syncytial Virus (RSV)

These assays offer rapid, point-of-care diagnostics, enabling healthcare providers to swiftly identify and differentiate between viral infections. This is especially critical for managing outbreaks, streamlining treatment decisions, and preventing the spread of highly contagious respiratory diseases.
